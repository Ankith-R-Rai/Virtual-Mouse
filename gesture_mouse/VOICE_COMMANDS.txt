══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  NOTES
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • All commands use FUZZY MATCHING — you don't have to say them
    exactly. Close enough works! (threshold: 72% similarity)
  • Two-pass matching: first token_sort_ratio (handles word reordering),
    then partial_ratio (handles substrings/extra words)
  • App names are separately fuzzy-matched (threshold: 75%)
  • Dynamic commands (search, type, open, draw, color) use regex
    patterns and are checked before the fuzzy dictionary match
  • Voice recognition requires an internet connection (Google Speech API)
  • If offline, falls back to CMU Sphinx (less accurate)
  • Total commands available: 72+ (including all phrase variations)
