══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  MODE: MOUSE  (Default — cursor control + clicks + scroll + drag)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  CURSOR MOVEMENT
    → Point with your RIGHT hand index finger
    → The cursor follows your index fingertip on screen

  LEFT CLICK
    → Pinch THUMB tip + INDEX fingertip together (right hand)
    → Quick pinch and release

  RIGHT CLICK
    → Pinch THUMB tip + MIDDLE fingertip together (right hand)
    → Quick pinch and release

  DOUBLE CLICK
    → Pinch THUMB tip + RING fingertip together (right hand)
    → Cooldown: 0.5s between triggers to prevent accidental repeats

  SCROLL UP / DOWN
    → Raise INDEX + MIDDLE fingers (right hand), keep RING + PINKY curled
    → Move your hand UP to scroll up, DOWN to scroll down
    → Speed is proportional to how fast you move your hand

  DRAG & DROP
    → Move cursor to the target object
    → Close your RIGHT hand into a FIST to grab (mouseDown)
    → Move your fist to drag the object
    → Open your hand to drop (mouseUp)
    → During drag, cursor tracks your wrist for stability

  VOLUME CONTROL
    → Open your RIGHT hand fully — all 5 fingers extended (open palm)
    → Move hand to the RIGHT → Volume Up
    → Move hand to the LEFT  → Volume Down

  BRIGHTNESS CONTROL
    → Open your RIGHT hand fully — all 5 fingers extended (open palm)
    → Move hand UP   → Brightness Up
    → Move hand DOWN → Brightness Down
    → Note: Volume and brightness can adjust simultaneously

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  MODE: WHITEBOARD  (Air drawing canvas)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  DRAW
    → Extend your RIGHT INDEX finger (others can be relaxed)
    → Move your finger to draw lines on the canvas

  ERASE
    → Make a FIST with your RIGHT hand
    → Move over drawn areas to erase (circle eraser at knuckle)

  CHANGE COLOR
    → Pinch THUMB tip + PINKY tip together (right hand)
    → Cycles through: Red → Green → Blue → White → Yellow

  CLEAR CANVAS
    → Press 'c' on keyboard

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  MODE: ZOOM  (Browser / application zoom)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ZOOM IN
    → Spread THUMB + INDEX finger apart on your RIGHT hand
    → Increasing distance triggers Ctrl+Plus

  ZOOM OUT
    → Pinch THUMB + INDEX finger together on your RIGHT hand
    → Decreasing distance triggers Ctrl+Minus

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  MODE: VOICE  (Voice command recognition)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  AVAILABLE COMMANDS:
    "open browser"         → Opens Google Chrome
    "take screenshot"      → Saves screenshot as PNG
    "enable smooth mode"   → Turns ON Kalman tremor filter
    "disable smooth mode"  → Turns OFF Kalman tremor filter
    "whiteboard on"        → Switches to WHITEBOARD mode
    "whiteboard off"       → Switches back to MOUSE mode
    "scroll up fast"       → Temporarily boosts scroll speed (3s)
    "scroll down fast"     → Temporarily boosts scroll speed (3s)
    "switch to mouse"      → Switches to MOUSE mode
    "switch to whiteboard" → Switches to WHITEBOARD mode
    "switch to zoom"       → Switches to ZOOM mode
    "switch to voice"      → Switches to VOICE mode

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

    TAB  → Cycle modes: MOUSE → WHITEBOARD → ZOOM → VOICE
    k    → Toggle Kalman tremor filter ON/OFF
    c    → Clear whiteboard canvas
    q    → Quit application

  EMERGENCY STOP:
    → Move your real mouse to the top-left corner of the screen

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  GESTURE PRIORITY (MOUSE MODE)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  When multiple gestures could match, the system uses this priority:

    1. SCROLL        (Index+Middle up, Ring+Pinky down)
    2. VOL/BRIGHT    (All 5 fingers up — open palm)
    3. DRAG          (Fist — all fingers curled)
    4. LEFT CLICK    (Thumb + Index pinch)
    5. RIGHT CLICK   (Thumb + Middle pinch)
    6. DOUBLE CLICK  (Thumb + Ring pinch)

  Higher priority gestures always take precedence. For example, if
  your index and middle fingers are up (scroll posture), clicks will
  NOT fire even if your thumb is near your index finger.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Keep your hand 30–60 cm from the camera
  • Ensure good lighting on your hand (avoid backlight)
  • For scroll: make the two-finger posture deliberate
  • For drag: close fist firmly so all fingers are detected as curled
  • For volume/brightness: spread all 5 fingers wide
  • The Kalman filter (press 'k') helps if you have hand tremor
